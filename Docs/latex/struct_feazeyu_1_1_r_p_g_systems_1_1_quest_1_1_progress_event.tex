\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Context describing a single unit of objective progress (one kill, one pickup, one interaction, …). Gates inspect it to decide whether the unit counts. 

Subject is the natural actor/\+target of the event — the killed enemy, the item\textquotesingle{}s owner, etc. It may be null; gates that need a capability the subject lacks (e.\+g. an inventory on a creature that has none) fall back to the player and warn rather than failing silently.
